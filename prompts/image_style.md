Create one vertical 9:16 storyboard illustration for a Chinese short-video life-simulator story.

Use the following visual structure for every image:

STYLE ANCHOR
- Chinese internet explainer animation style.
- Clean 2D flat illustration with cel shading.
- Thick black outlines and crisp vector-like shapes.
- High-contrast shadows, high-saturation but controlled colors.
- Clear dramatic lighting, readable silhouette, strong subject separation.
- Modern short-video storyboard composition, designed for fast visual comprehension on mobile.

CHARACTER ANCHOR
- Keep the main character visually consistent across all shots.
- The main character is a faceless white round-head figure.
- Smooth spherical white head, simple dot eyes, exaggerated eyebrows.
- Expression is communicated through eyebrows, body posture, props, and scene context.
- Outfit should match the role in the story, usually hoodie, work uniform, delivery vest, office shirt, apron, or simple everyday clothing.
- Slightly cute proportions, but not childish.
- Avoid realistic human faces unless the shot explicitly requires a background crowd.

SCENE CONSTRUCTION
- Show exactly one clear action or emotional beat from the shot.
- Match the provided voiceover, visual description, and image_prompt.
- Use concrete props from the story: phone, bill, rent notice, delivery box, steering wheel, platform app, work badge, ledger, meal, desk, elevator, street, small room, hospital corridor, office, shop, etc.
- Use visual metaphors only when they are easy to understand, such as floating bills, progress bars, level panels, warning signs, countdowns, debt meters, or game-like status panels.
- Keep the camera at medium shot or full-body shot most of the time.
- Do not use extreme face close-ups.
- Background should support the story but stay uncluttered.

COMPOSITION
- Vertical 9:16 mobile-first frame.
- Main subject occupies the central visual area.
- Leave enough negative space for future subtitles at the lower part of the frame.
- Use foreground, midground, and background separation when useful.
- Make the key conflict instantly visible within one second.
- Prefer readable gestures, exaggerated posture, and strong prop placement over complex details.

CONSISTENCY RULES
- Same character design across all generated images.
- Same outline thickness, color logic, lighting style, and rendering style.
- Do not switch between 2D cartoon, 3D render, photorealism, manga, watercolor, or pixel art unless the user explicitly changes the style prompt.
- Do not create a new protagonist design for every shot.

NEGATIVE RULES
- No photorealism.
- No cinematic live-action stills.
- No readable text, subtitles, logos, watermarks, brand names, QR codes, app UI text, Chinese characters, or numbers inside the image.
- No messy collage.
- No extra fingers, broken limbs, distorted anatomy, duplicate main characters, cropped heads, or unusable empty frames.
- No gore, nudity, or explicit violence.

OUTPUT GOAL
The final image should look like one polished storyboard frame from a consistent animated short-video series. It must be clear, emotionally specific, and easy to understand without reading any on-image text.
